03 / Insights

What the evidence says

InsightEvidence
Smart rings are bought for sleep and recovery, not as miniature sports watches. Oura and Samsung retail review themes repeatedly surface sleep tracking, comfort, and battery.
Subscription-free positioning is a real wedge, but it cannot compensate for unreliable hardware. RingConn claims no subscription; Reddit and Walmart discussions highlight battery and support risks in adjacent products.
